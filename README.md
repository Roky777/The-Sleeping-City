# 🌧️ The Sleeping City

> _"Some cities sleep. Others are forgotten."_

A cinematic puzzle-platformer built with **Phaser 3** where a lone hooded child journeys through a rain-soaked world to restore power to a forgotten city buried beneath the earth.

Inspired by **Inside**, **Limbo**, **Journey**, **Little Nightmares**, **Planet of Lana**, and the emotional atmosphere of **Interstellar**, _The Sleeping City_ combines environmental storytelling, electricity-based puzzles, and subtle guidance systems to create a short but unforgettable experience.

---

## 🎮 About the Game

The city sleeps.

Its streets are silent.
Its lights have faded.
Its people remain trapped in an unnatural stillness.

You play as a mysterious hooded child who ventures through ruined forests and forgotten underground chambers to uncover what silenced the city and restore the heartbeat hidden beneath its darkness.

There are no enemies to defeat.

No weapons to wield.

Only curiosity, observation, ingenuity, and hope.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🌧 Atmospheric Exploration

- Rain-soaked environments
- Cinematic title sequence
- Environmental storytelling
- Immersive ambience and sound design
- Minimalist narrative presentation

### 🐱 The Sleeping Cat Sequence

- Discover a mysterious sleeping cat
- Attempt to wake it through interaction
- Collect scattered toy mouse parts
- Repair a forgotten toy mouse
- Solve a closed-circuit puzzle
- Awaken the cat through ingenuity
- Watch it guide your journey forward

### ⚡ Electricity-Based Puzzles

The game gradually teaches concepts through interaction:

- Circuit completion
- Power restoration
- Hidden pathways
- Conductors vs insulators
- Timing-based hazards
- Observation and experimentation

### 🧩 Progressive Puzzle Design

Every puzzle follows the philosophy:

> **Observe → Experiment → Learn → Solve**

No mechanic is introduced without first teaching it naturally.

### 💡 Accessibility-Focused Hint System

- Optional hint button
- Appears only if the player remains stuck
- Progressive hints
- Respects player intelligence
- Prevents frustration without spoiling discovery

### 🎬 Cinematic Storytelling

Story is conveyed through:

- The environment
- Small interactions
- Visual details
- Atmosphere and music
- Minimal but meaningful subtitles

# 📖 Gameplay Flow

## 🌲 Outside World

- Explore the rain-soaked forest.
- Discover the sleeping cat.
- Attempt to wake it.
- Collect:
  - 🔋 Battery
  - 🐭 Mouse Head
  - 🐭 Mouse Tail

- Repair the Toy Mouse.
- Solve the Closed Circuit Puzzle.
- Activate the toy mouse.
- The cat awakens.
- The cat chases the toy mouse.
- The cat leads you toward the glowing tree.
- Solve the Tree Pipe Puzzle.
- Restore ancient power.
- Reveal the path below.

---

## 🌌 The Descent

- Enter the hidden passage.
- Fall through the depths beneath the world.
- Witness fleeting glimpses of the sleeping city.
- Follow the fading light.

---

## 🏙 Inner World

### Puzzle 1 – The Pit

Restore electricity to reveal hidden safe paths.

**Concepts Learned**

- Electricity reveals truth.
- Observation matters.

---

### Puzzle 2 – The Electrified Pond

Navigate dangerous currents using insulation and timing.

**Concepts Learned**

- Electricity can protect.
- Electricity can harm.
- Timing is everything.

---

## ❤️ Final Level – The Heart of the City

Descend into the city's forgotten core.

Restore dormant generators.

Use every lesson learned throughout your journey.

Awaken the machine that once powered an entire civilization.

Because this is not simply another puzzle.

It is the city's heartbeat.

---

## 🌆 Ending

As power surges through the Heart of the City:

- Street lamps flicker back to life.
- Apartment windows begin to glow.
- Clocks resume ticking.
- Trams hum once more.
- Fireflies gather beneath the ancient tree.
- The city remembers itself.

Final words:

> **"The city remembered."**

# 🧠 Design Philosophy

The Sleeping City is built around four principles:

## Curiosity Over Complexity

Puzzles invite experimentation instead of overwhelming the player.

---

## Atmosphere Over Exposition

The world tells its own story.

---

## Emotion Through Interaction

Meaning emerges through actions rather than dialogue.

---

## Respect the Player

Guidance exists only when needed.

Discovery always comes first.

# 🚀 Getting Started

## Clone the repository

```bash
git clone https://github.com/Roky777/The-Sleeping-City.git
```

## Navigate into the project

```bash
cd The-Sleeping-City
```

## Install dependencies

```bash
npm install
```

## Start the development server

```bash
npm run dev
```

## Build for production

```bash
npm run build
```

Open the generated local URL in your browser and begin your journey.
